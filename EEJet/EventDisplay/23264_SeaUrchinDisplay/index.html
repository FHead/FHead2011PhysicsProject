Controls
  • You may need to click on the display area to focus before keys could work.
  • Moving: "ASDF" or arrow keys to move around. We can also drag with left mouse button.
  • Rotate: "Q"/"PageUp" to rotate left, "E"/"PageDown" to rotate right
  • Zooming: "R" or "home" to zoom in, "F" or "end" to zoom out. We can also use mouse middle button scroll.
  • Right click and save image!
Display guide
  • Yellow ball is the origin
  • Axes are (red = x, green = y, blue = z).
  • The knots are at 1, 5, 10, 25, 100 GeV. Darker knots are negative.
  • Note that there is some camera distortion when distances are large.
Particle format
  • Each line is one particle
  • Each particle contains four numbers separated by space (and/or comma): 3-momentum, and ID.
  • Add a "#" to indicate comments. Those lines will be ignored
  • The ID is used to determine the line color, and it goes from 1 to 6. If you need more colors let Yi know.
